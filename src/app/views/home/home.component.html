Welcome to Sullivan's Heritage Barbeque!

Here at Sullivan’s Heritage Barbeque we take the time to make our Eastern North Carolina barbeque an experience, not just a meal. Our meats, fresh daily, are smoked and grilled slowly using locally sourced products of the highest quality. Join us for great food, a fun experience and superb service. We hope that you’ll bring your families, relax at our picnic tables or take it to go. No matter what - we want you to feel welcomed and a part of our family and community.

-- Robert and Darlene Sullivan

* Find us at the intersection of route 41 and route 10 – next to the consignment shop.
